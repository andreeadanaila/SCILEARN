The Sun: Our Brillian Star

❮ ❯


Question: What is the sun?

The sun is a gigantic ball of hot gases, mainly hydrogen and helium. It's like a giant fire burning in space!

Question: What does the sun look like?

The sun appears as a big, round, glowing ball of light in the sky. Sometimes, you might see dark spots on its surface called sunspots.

Question: What are some things the sun does?

The sun gives us light and warmth, helps plants grow, and makes weather like wind and rain.

Question: How old is the sun?

The sun is about 4.6 billion years old!

Question: How big is the sun compared to Earth?

The sun is much bigger than Earth. You could fit more than a million Earths inside it!

Question: What are some ways to stay safe in the sun?

Wear sunscreen to protect your skin, sunglasses to shield your eyes, and drink plenty of water to stay hydrated!
